Rogaliki

Directions
  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, and cold butter.
  2. Add yogurt and egg yolks; knead into dough.
  3. Chill dough for 1 hour.
  4. Roll dough, cut into triangles, and add jam.
  5. Roll into crescents and bake until golden.
History of the Dish

Rogaliki are traditional Eastern European pastries made from yeast dough and filled with jam, nuts, or poppy seeds. As immigrants arrived in America, the recipe evolved into rugelach, using cream cheese dough and becoming more cookie-like.

Ingredients
  • Dough
  • 2 cups + 2 tsp flour
  • 1 tsp dried yeast
  • 1 1/8 stick cold butter
  • 5 tbsp yogurt or sour cream
  • 2 egg yolks
  • Pinch of salt
  • Filling
  • 5 tbsp jam (any flavor)
